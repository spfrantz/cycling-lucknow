Cycling Lucknow

Welcome! I was fortunate to spend 2018 based in Lucknow as a fellow of the American Institute of Indian Studies. During this time I learned a thing or two about cycling in the city. I hope this modest site will be useful to others interested in exploring this storied place and its surrounding countryside by bicycle.

I have no immediate plans to return to Lucknow and do not intend to maintain these pages. The city is growing very rapidly and the information presented here will soon be out of date. However, if you have additions or corrections, please open a GitHub issue or send an e-mail and I will consider making a revision.

These pages were created as an exercise in HTML & CSS for CS50W, which is part of the excellent (and free!) CS50 series of HarvardX courses. I encourage anyone with an interest in computer science, programming, or just seeking to understand modern computing to check out CS50.
