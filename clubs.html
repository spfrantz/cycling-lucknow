Clubs & Group Rides

There are a number of opportunities for joining organized rides:

  • Pedalyatri Lucknow: organizes early-morning social rides of varying distances and paces, several days per week. Ride plans shared via WhatsApp. [Instagram] [Strava]
  • Decathalon: occassional rides from the store. Shared via Facebook.
  • Shunty Cycle Store: occassional rides from the shop. Shared via Facebook.
  • Audax India Randonneurs: organizes endurance rides across India, including Lucknow.
